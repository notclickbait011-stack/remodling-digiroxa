Our Process

A renovation should feel composed, not chaotic.

From the first walkthrough of your Greenwich home to the final white-glove reveal, every stage is mapped, scheduled, and overseen by a single dedicated lead — so you always know exactly where your project stands.

Four Considered Stages

One path, mapped
before we begin.

Most remodeling stress comes from the unknown — shifting timelines, surprise costs, and contractors who go quiet. We replace all of it with structure: four defined stages, a fixed proposal, weekly updates, and a lead who answers the phone. Here is exactly how your project unfolds.

  1. 01

    Consultation

    An on-site visit to understand your home, your habits, and your budget — at no cost.

  2. 02

    Design & Planning

    Plans, renderings, materials, and a fixed proposal refined until the vision is exact.

  3. 03

    Construction

    Daily oversight, immaculate jobsites, and a transparent schedule you can follow.

  4. 04

    Reveal & Warranty

    A white-glove walkthrough, a complete project binder, and a multi-year warranty.

Stage 01 — Consultation

We start in your home, not a showroom.

Every project begins with a complimentary on-site consultation. We walk your spaces, listen to how your household actually lives, and study the constraints and opportunities the architecture presents — light, flow, structure, and the details you've lived with for years.

Before we leave, we'll share a candid view of scope, realistic budget ranges for your goals across Fairfield County, and the likely timeline. There's no pressure and no obligation — just clarity about whether we're the right firm for your home.

  • On-site walkthrough
  • Goals & lifestyle interview
  • Honest budget ranges
  • Feasibility review
  • Preliminary timeline
  • No-cost, no-obligation
Book your consultation
Stage 02 — Design & Planning

The vision, resolved to the last detail.

Our in-house design team translates your goals into measured plans, photorealistic renderings, and a curated material palette — cabinetry, stone, tile, fixtures, and finishes you can see and touch before a single wall is opened.

We handle engineering, permitting, and town approvals across Greenwich and the surrounding towns, then issue a fixed, itemized proposal. Every line is transparent, so the number you approve is the number you pay — barring changes you choose to make.

  • Measured plans & 3D renderings
  • Curated material selections
  • Engineering & permitting
  • Fixed, itemized proposal
  • Locked production schedule
  • Single point of contact
Explore financing options
Stage 03 — Construction

Built by our own craftsmen, watched daily.

When construction begins, a dedicated site lead manages the day-to-day so you never have to coordinate trades. We protect your home with dust barriers and floor coverings, confine work to agreed hours, and leave the jobsite broom-clean every evening.

You receive a written update every week with photos, completed milestones, and what's ahead. Most clients stay comfortably at home throughout — and many tell us the quietest part of the project was the construction itself.

  • Dedicated on-site lead
  • Dust & floor protection
  • Weekly written updates
  • Immaculate daily cleanup
  • Quality checks at each phase
  • Agreed, respectful work hours
See completed projects
Stage 04 — Reveal & Warranty

The keys to a home transformed.

We close with a white-glove walkthrough, reviewing every detail together and resolving any final punch-list items before we consider the work complete. You receive a complete project binder — warranties, finish schedules, paint formulas, and care guidance for every surface.

Our relationship doesn't end at handover. Your project is backed by a multi-year workmanship warranty, and we remain a phone call away long after the last contractor has gone home.

  • White-glove walkthrough
  • Final punch-list resolution
  • Complete project binder
  • Finish & care documentation
  • Multi-year warranty
  • Responsive aftercare
