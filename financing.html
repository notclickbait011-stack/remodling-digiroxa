Home Improvement Loan

7.9% APR

A fixed-rate, unsecured loan designed specifically for renovation. Predictable monthly payments and no collateral required — ideal for mid-size kitchen and bathroom projects.

  • Fixed rates from 7.9% APR
  • Terms from 24 to 144 months
  • No collateral required
  • Funds available in days

Deferred-Interest Plan

0% Intro

Begin work now and pay no interest during the promotional period. A smart fit for homeowners planning to pay the balance within the introductory window.

  • 0% interest intro period
  • No payments while planning
  • Pay in full to skip interest
  • Quick online approval

HELOC Partner Program

Variable

Tap the equity you've built for larger, whole-home transformations and additions. Draw what you need, when you need it, with competitive variable rates through our lending partners.

  • Borrow against home equity
  • Draw funds as the work progresses
  • Competitive variable rates
  • Best for whole-home & additions
