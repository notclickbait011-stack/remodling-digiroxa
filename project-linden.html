Case Study

The Linden Suite

Darien, CT — a serene primary suite and spa bath opening onto a private terrace, created across 1,100 square feet over four quiet, exacting months.

Get Free Consultation Call 800.555.1234
Overview

The owners wanted one thing from their renovation: a true retreat at the end of the day. Their existing primary suite was generous in size but generic in feeling — a builder bedroom, a dated bath, and a flat roof over the garage that begged to become something more. We reshaped the entire upper wing into a calm, light-washed suite with a spa bath and a private terrace overlooking the trees.

The Transformation

A bedroom made into a retreat.

We reorganized the wing around a clear sequence — bedroom, dressing room, then spa bath — each step quieter and more private than the last. A new set of glass doors opens the bath and bedroom onto a terrace built atop the garage roof, turning an unused surface into the suite's most beloved feature.

Back to portfolio

The Brief

After years of early mornings and long commutes, the owners wanted their primary suite to feel like a small, private hotel — somewhere to decompress, not just sleep. The brief emphasized calm above all: soft natural light, warm tactile materials, a spa bath that didn't feel clinical, and, if at all possible, a way to step outside without going downstairs.

The Approach

The existing footprint was sound, so our work was about refinement rather than expansion. We re-planned the suite into a gentle procession of spaces, relocated the entry to create a proper dressing room, and reframed the flat garage roof to carry a waterproof terrace deck. In the bath, we specified a freestanding soaking tub set against a window, a generous wet zone with a rain shower, and honed stone underfoot warmed by radiant heat.

The palette was kept deliberately quiet — pale plaster, warm oak, and soft limestone — so the room recedes and the daylight and the treetops do the work. Every fixture, niche, and switch was placed by hand on site to keep the detailing serene and uncluttered.

"It's the first room we walk into at night and the last we want to leave in the morning. It feels like ours in a way no house ever has."

The Result

The Linden Suite is now exactly what the owners asked for — a genuine retreat. The spa bath is calm and warm, the dressing room finally makes mornings effortless, and the terrace has become a nightly ritual. Four months of quiet, precise work delivered a space that feels both luxurious and deeply restful.

Work Performed

  • Full reconfiguration of the upper-wing primary suite
  • New dressing room with custom cabinetry and integrated lighting
  • Spa bath with freestanding soaking tub and curbless rain shower
  • Radiant-heated honed limestone flooring throughout the bath
  • Reframed garage roof and new waterproof private terrace deck
  • New glass terrace doors from both bedroom and bath
  • Relocated plumbing, new HVAC zoning, and layered lighting design
  • Warm plaster walls, oak millwork, and unlacquered brass fittings
