One Continuous Home

Extending the home into the landscape.

An outdoor room shouldn't feel like an afterthought tacked onto the back door. We align sightlines, materials, and ceiling heights with the interior so stepping outside feels like moving from one room to the next.

  • Flush interior-to-exterior thresholds
  • Matched stone & flooring palettes
  • Framed garden & water views
  • Continuous ceiling & lighting lines
  • Wide-opening glass & sliders
  • Cohesive furniture zoning

Built To Last

Engineered for New England seasons.

Coastal salt, hard freezes, and heavy snow punish outdoor construction. We specify materials and detailing that shrug off Connecticut winters and look as good in year ten as in year one.

  • Frost-depth footings & drainage
  • Marine-grade & powder-coated metals
  • Sealed natural & porcelain stone
  • Snow-load-rated structures
  • Winterizable plumbing & gas
  • Low-maintenance hardwoods
